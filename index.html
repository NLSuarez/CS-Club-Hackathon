Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is usually a 10 to 36-hour event where developers, companies, teachers, and all kinds of people from various backgrounds come together and compete in a Software/Hardware competition.

Typically, teams of four are formed and they try to develop an application, website, Arduino project, robotics, or even just to go learn.

The grand prizes usually range from a few thousand dollars to micro controllers, companies investing in ideas, and even interviews at software companies.

Many companies also go to hackathons to simply see the local talent and recruit software developers, graphic designers, etc.

Yes, it is called a “Hack”-a-thon, but it does not have to deal with Computer Hacking. They call it a hackathon because people go to hack something together.

Much like making a Frankenstein project or coding up something so horrible that you are even surprised that it works.

What if I am new to programming or I am not even a coder?

You should still definitely go! A lot of employers look for CS students that are motivated and always willing to step outside of their comfort zones.

Just give it a shot! CS Students go to hackathons all the time without knowing how to code and come out wanting to learn more and having a better understanding of what software development is.

It is not about winning, but rather what you can learn along the way!

Will there be any prizes?
Yes, there will be prizes for 1st to 3rd place. Although, it isn’t all about winning but what you learn and build during those 24 hours. There will be a panel of judges that will rate which are the best hacks based on creativity, technical difficulty, design, and presentation. Prizes are TBA.
What should I bring?
Please bring a valid university ID, a computer/laptop, chargers, a power strip and any hardware you will use for your hack. You can also bring a sleeping bag, pillow, toiletries, and a couple changes of clothes, food will be provided at the event. No firearms, weapons, alcohol, or illegal drugs are allowed!
What are the size of the teams?
The teams are a maximum of 4 people. You can start off the event with a team already formed or you can meet with people at the event who also don’t have a team. Don’t worry about taking either approach; just show up.
